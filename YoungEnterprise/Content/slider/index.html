The Awards Ceronomy of the 2012 Club championships will take place on the 26th of January at the Choir hall next to the lesuire centre, followed by the annual Disco!
Caldicot Swimming Club would like to offer all new members a warm welcome. Our club has developed over the years and now has a substantial membership from juniors to masters. We pride our selves in being a friendly relaxed club with a high standard of coaching. We have an improvers section for those children aged 5 and older who can just swim but need to build their technique as well as their fitness and endurance. Our masters section caters for competitive adults, triathletes and swim fit.
Don't forget Swimming starts back after Christmas on Monday the 7th of January!
