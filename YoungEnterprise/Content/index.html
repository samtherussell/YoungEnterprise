Welcome To Eagle Events.


Eagle Events is a the new revolutionary events management service based in south wales, we aim to provide local events for the local community at an affordable price. The business itself is run and managed by students as part of a 'Young Enterprise' scheme which aims to try and get young people into business. We aim to show that we can succeed and compete in a real life market place, and do so successfully.


Our Services


Eagle Events aims to plan and run a variety of local events to benefit the community and bring people together to have a good time, events we may run:

  • Parties for school kids.
  • Film screenings.
  • Local fêtes.
  • Sports Tournaments.
